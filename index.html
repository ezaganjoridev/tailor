Toronto Tailoring FAQ

Answers about appointments, pants hemming, suit alterations, bridal fittings, leather, denim, curtains, rush service, and pricing.

Do I need an appointment for alterations?

Appointments are recommended so we can set aside fitting time, especially for suits, bridal gowns, eveningwear, and complex alterations.

How long do alterations take?

Turnaround depends on complexity. Most standard alterations are 3–7 days, with rush options available.

Do you do pants hemming in Toronto?

Yes. Yorkdale Tailoring offers standard pants hemming, original hem jeans hemming, Euro hems, tapering, and waist adjustments at 135 Marlee Avenue.

Can you alter suits and formalwear?

Yes. We tailor suit jackets, trousers, waistcoats, formal dresses, bridesmaid dresses, and eveningwear with attention to structure, drape, and movement.

Can you work with luxury fabrics?

Yes. Wool, silk, cashmere, leather, suede, and designer garments are handled with appropriate techniques for the fabric and construction.

Do you alter leather, suede, and denim?

Yes. We offer leather and suede alterations, original-hem jeans hemming, denim tapering, waist adjustments, and repairs for jackets, pants, skirts, and accessories.

Do you alter curtains and home textiles?

Yes. We provide curtain hemming, shortening, relining, and alterations for drapes, table linens, cushion covers, and other home textiles.

Do you offer rush alterations?

Yes. Same-day and next-day rush service may be available depending on the garment, fabric, and work required. Rush fees apply.

How accurate is the pricing?

We provide accurate quotes after a quick fitting. Final pricing reflects fabric, lining, and finishing detail.
